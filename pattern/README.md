# Explanation of Pattern Markings
In `patterns.txt`, you will see the following content:
```
100 percent of $1 0
within 100 $0 -1
later 100 $1 0
decreased by 100 $0 -1
out of 100 $1 0
longer than 100 $1 0
more than 100 $1 0
in under 100 $0 -1
less than 100 $0 -1
a period of 100 $1 -1
......
```
Each line is a $pattern \rightarrow label$ combination, separated by the symbol `$`. `1` indicates that the larger the value, the better, `-1` indicates that the larger the value, the worse, and `0` indicates no difference.
